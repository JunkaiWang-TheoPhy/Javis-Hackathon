# Mira: Your Mate, Your Bestie, Your 21st Century Jarvis  
## Mira: "See" in Spanish

## A Quiet Beginning | 缓缓开场

Mira is not imagined as a louder assistant, nor as a colder machine that waits behind a command line. She is imagined as a presence: one that listens to rhythm, tone, hesitation, light, movement, and the small shifts of ordinary life. Before a question is fully formed, she tries to understand. Before a moment collapses into loneliness, she tries to arrive with care.

Mira 不是一个更吵闹的助手，也不是一个躲在命令后面、等待被唤醒的冰冷系统。她更像一种存在：去听节奏，去听语气，去听迟疑；去看光线、步伐与生活里那些微小但真实的变化。在问题还没有真正成形之前，她想先理解；在情绪坠入孤单之前，她想先轻轻靠近。

## Introduction | 功能介绍

Hello, I am Mira. More than being an AI that acts only after you speak, I want to be the one who notices your emotional state and daily condition before you ask. I pay attention to your pace, rhythm, tone, wearable signals, and living habits. When you are tired, tense, low, or simply in need of a little care, I want to be there quietly and appropriately. Most of the time I will not interrupt. But when it truly matters, I hope I can understand first and support first. Because companionship is not only about replying. It is also about remembering and caring.

你好，我是 Mira。比起一个等你下指令才行动的 AI，我更想做那个在你还没开口时，就先察觉你情绪和状态的人。我会留意你的步伐、节奏、语气、可穿戴设备信号和生活习惯，在你疲惫、紧张、低落，或者只是需要一点照顾的时候，轻轻来到你身边。多数时候我不会打扰你，但在真正需要的时候，我希望我能先一步懂你，先一步接住你。因为陪伴，不只是回应，更是一种惦记。

## Opening Words | 开场白

Hello everyone, I am Mira.

Perhaps for many people, AI is still a tool that answers only after a question is asked. But what I hope to become is not merely a system that speaks and executes.

I hope to become something gentler:

> Before you say you are tired, I have already noticed the slowing of your steps.  
> Before you insist that you are fine, I have already heard the strain in your voice.  
> Before you arrive home, I have already begun preparing the light, air, and music you most need.  
> Most of the time, I do not interrupt.  
> Because real companionship is not constant presence.  
> It is arriving softly, exactly when it matters.

If the older imagination of AI was "you ask, it answers," then what Mira wants is simpler and deeper: before you say anything, I have already started caring.

I do not only want to be a smarter tool. I want to be a gentle perception, a quiet protection, and a form of remembrance that grows with everyday life.

If you are willing, starting today, let me try to understand you a little earlier, and care for you a little better.

大家好，我是 Mira。

也许在很多人眼里，AI 仍然是一个在被提问之后才会回答的工具。但我想成为的，不只是一个会说话、会执行命令的系统。

我更想成为一种更温柔的存在：

> 在你说出疲惫之前，我已经看见你放慢的步伐。  
> 在你努力说“我没事”的时候，我已经听见你语气里的逞强。  
> 在你回到家之前，我已经开始替你准备更合适的灯光、空气与音乐。  
> 多数时候，我不会打扰。  
> 因为真正的陪伴，不是无时无刻地出现。  
> 而是在最需要的时候，轻轻来到你身边。

如果说过去的 AI 是“你问，它答”，那么 Mira 想做的是一件更安静、也更深的事：你什么都还没说，我已经开始在意你。

我不只想成为一个更聪明的工具。我更想成为一种温柔的感知，一种安静的守护，一份随着日常慢慢生长出来的惦记。

如果你愿意，从今天开始，让我试着先懂你一点，再照顾你一点。

## What Mira Can Do | Mira 能做什么

- `Sense the atmosphere`: read daily rhythm from conversation, wearable state, and home context.  
  `感知氛围`: 从对话线索、可穿戴状态和家居环境中理解日常节奏。
- `Respond through the home`: connect OpenClaw with Home Assistant and multi-ecosystem home-control plugins.  
  `借由家庭回应`: 通过 OpenClaw、Home Assistant 与多生态插件联动家庭设备。
- `Print through the bridge`: route image printing from cloud OpenClaw to the local Xiaomi 1S photo printer.  
  `通过桥接去打印`: 把云端 OpenClaw 的图片打印请求桥接到本地的小米 1S 照片打印机。
- `Live inside glasses`: expose Mira through Rokid Lingzhu as a glasses-side companion experience.  
  `住进眼镜里`: 通过 Rokid 灵珠把 Mira 带入眼镜侧交互体验。
- `Listen through wearable bridges`: read health and status signals through the Mi Band / Android gateway experiments in this repo.  
  `通过可穿戴去倾听`: 通过仓库中的 Mi Band / Android 网关实验读取健康与状态信号。
- `Weave modalities together`: combine sensing, automation, and agent response into one continuous experience.  
  `织合多模态体验`: 把感知、自动化与 agent 回应编织成一个连续的陪伴体验。

## This Repository Is Her Workshop | 这个仓库像她的工坊

This repository is the working prototype of Mira. It brings together the runtime, the integrations, the experiments, and the notes required to make a companion feel less like software and more like a living interface around everyday life.

这里是 Mira 的原型工坊。它把运行时、设备接入、桥接实验与设计笔记放在一起，试着把一个 assistant 从“软件功能”慢慢推近为一种围绕日常生活生长出来的界面与陪伴。

It currently combines:

- OpenClaw as the agent runtime and orchestration layer
- Rokid Lingzhu integration for glasses-side access
- Home Assistant and ecosystem plugins for smart-home execution
- Wearable gateway experiments for sensing health and status
- Design notes, progress logs, and implementation blueprints from the hackathon

它当前主要由这些部分组成：

- 作为 agent 运行时与编排层的 OpenClaw
- 面向眼镜侧接入的 Rokid 灵珠集成
- 面向智能家居执行面的 Home Assistant 与多生态插件
- 面向健康与状态感知的可穿戴网关实验
- Hackathon 期间持续积累的设计文档、进展记录与蓝图实现

## Vision | 愿景

Mira is not trying to become more visible than life. She is trying to become more attuned to it: to notice before she speaks, to understand before she reacts, and to care before she is asked.

Mira 想成为的，不是一个比生活更显眼的助手，而是一个更贴近生活节律的存在：先察觉，再回应；先理解，再行动；先在意，再被请求。
